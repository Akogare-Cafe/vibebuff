## Best Practices

### Before Sending
1. **Personalize** - Reference specific content from their site
2. **Research** - Know who you're emailing and why
3. **Value first** - Lead with what you can offer them
4. **Keep it short** - Respect their time
5. **Clear CTA** - One specific ask per email

### Follow-Up Schedule
- **Day 3-5**: First follow-up if no response
- **Day 10-14**: Second follow-up
- **Day 21+**: Final follow-up, then move on

### Follow-Up Template

```
Hi [Name],

Just following up on my previous email about [topic].

[One sentence reminder of your ask]

No worries if it's not a fit - just let me know either way.

Thanks,
[Your Name]
```

### Tracking
Track all outreach in a spreadsheet:
- Date sent
- Contact name/email
- Site/publication
- Template used
- Response status
- Follow-up dates
- Outcome
